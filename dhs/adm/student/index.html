Admission Form

Before Proceed Please Read these instructions carefully
  1. Fillup the admission form carefully. Mandatory Fields are marked with a .
  2. Photo and Signature size should not exceed 20KB and Other Document size should not exceed 250KB.
    Photo and Date of Birth Proof / Birth Certificate is Mandatory for all.
  3. Once Submitted, no more Editing options are there, so check all fields before submit.
  4. After successful Submission, Write down the Application ID for further LogIn to your own Application.
  5. On the next Page, click on "Login" and enter Application ID as login ID and Date of Birth as Password.
  6. Students applying for Class IX should submit their signature, otherwise Admit Card without signature will not be accepted at admission test.
